# PART 2: 7-Day Launch Sequence
## Not a Countdown. A Cultural Moment.
## Each day = a mini-viral moment designed to create curiosity, participation, and FOMO

> Boring launches: "Day 1: Teaser. Day 2: Reveal. Day 3: Please download."
> Our launch: Every single day is designed to be screenshot-worthy, share-worthy, talk-about-it-at-chai-worthy.
> Inspired by: Swiggy WITASA (800K participants), CRED launch (nobody understood it, everyone talked about it), Duolingo dead owl (pure chaos marketing).

---

## Pre-Launch Setup (Before Day 1)

- Seed 5-10 parent "insiders" with early access (they'll post organically on Day 4-5)
- Create a WhatsApp Broadcast list of 200+ education-interested contacts
- Prepare all 7 days of content in advance (no scrambling)
- Brief any friendly teacher/principal accounts for Day 3 reshare
