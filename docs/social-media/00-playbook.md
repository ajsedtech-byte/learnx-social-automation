# LearnX Social Media Playbook
## Based on what ACTUALLY worked for Indian brands

> Not theory. Every pattern here has a real viral post behind it with real numbers.

---

## The LearnX Voice = Zomato Wit + PhysicsWallah Heart

We're the **education brand that doesn't sound like an education brand.** Parents expect preachy — we give them funny. Students expect boring — we give them relatable. That tension between expectation and reality is our entire strategy.

---

## 8 Proven Patterns We Use (with source examples)

### Pattern 1: SELF-DEPRECATING HUMOR
**Source:** Zomato "ghar ka khana" (19K likes, 4K RTs — a food app telling people to eat at home)
**Our version:** An education app admitting students don't actually study for 4 hours. Being honest about what we know. Making fun of ourselves.

### Pattern 2: REAL DATA AS HUMOR
**Source:** Blinkit "2025 in a Blink" (IIM-A 60,000 printouts, viral nationally)
**Our version:** "LearnX Data Drops" — real anonymized app stats presented with cheeky copy. Weekly series.

### Pattern 3: BOLLYWOOD/CULTURAL HIJACKING
**Source:** Zomato IPO "dhak dhak" (22K likes), Blinkit "doodh mangoge" (100K+ likes)
**Our version:** Education-twist on famous Bollywood dialogues, meme templates, and trending moments.

### Pattern 4: MOMENT MARKETING (SPEED)
**Source:** Amul topicals (60+ years, published within hours), Fevicol + Ronaldo ("Na bottle hategi")
**Our version:** Within 4 hours of ANY trend — IPL, exam results, viral moments — LearnX reacts with education twist.

### Pattern 5: PARTICIPATION / CURIOSITY GAP
**Source:** Swiggy "Why Is This A Swiggy Ad?" (800K participants), Swiggy Voice of Hunger (150K entries)
**Our version:** "Wrong Answers Only", polls, challenges, "finish this sentence" — make the audience the content.

### Pattern 6: PERSONALIZED SHAREABLE STATS
**Source:** Spotify Wrapped (60M people voluntarily shared branded content)
**Our version:** "LearnX Wrapped" — monthly personalized cards parents share because it's about THEIR child.

### Pattern 7: RAW/ANTI-TEMPLATE FORMAT
**Source:** Netflix India meme-first strategy (300K-500K likes per post), notes-app confessions format
**Our version:** Raw text tweets, student confessions, deliberately unpolished posts that feel like a person, not a brand.

### Pattern 8: CROSS-BRAND / CULTURAL BANTER
**Source:** Durex + Netflix + Tinder Valentine's banter, Zomato congratulating Swiggy IPO
**Our version:** Engage with trending brands, respond to other education-related posts with wit, be part of conversations.
